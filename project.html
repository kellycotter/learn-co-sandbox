About the Coders:

This website was created by Kelly Cotter, Kira Hartness and Ella Sklar! We all live in Massachusetts and we grew up loving our city, Boston! We have spent our childhood watching Tom Brady throw spirals and seeing the "The Daughters of Edward Darley Boit" by John Singer Sargent at the Museum of Fine Arts Boston. We wanted to teach our users about all the great things to do in Boston! We hope you find our website useful during your next visit to Boston!

Our Inspiration

During our time at the Kode with Klossy summer camp in Boston we found that our instructors (who were from Hawaii and Baltimore) did not know what to do in Boston over the weekend! However, when they went to websites like Yelp only showed the basic attractions unlike the hidden gems that are located in Boston. Therefore, the three scholars, Kelly, Kira and Ella decided to create a project that can help people like our instructors find great places to visit based on their preferences.
